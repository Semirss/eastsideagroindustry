Get to Know Us &
Our Story

EastSide Agro Industry PLC was established in Ethiopia under the commercial registration and business license proclamation number 980/2016 so as to contribute in the Agro Processing and Import-Export business in the most ethical and professional style. We mainly focus on growing and exporting original Ethiopian Coffee with maximum care for quality and novelty.

Our Company is backed by reputable and well experienced team that ensures the market conveniently accessed and get the best quality coffee from the source at the right time. We promote working closely with farmers to ensure the production of high quality coffee. We strongly believe our passion to the business, quality control system and service commitment to our clients to enable them get the best coffee.

Our Mission
Our mission is YOU - our clients! We sweat day and night to deliver the best coffee product Ethiopia offers to satisfy you 100%.

Our Vision
Our vision is to be the best coffee exporting company from the land of coffee origin, Ethiopia, to the rest of the world.
Our Values
We prioritize dedication and quality! We work to lead the coffee export industry with unwavering and wholesome work culture.
